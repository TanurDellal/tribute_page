Cillian Murphy

Best Performing Irish Actor

Timeline of Cillian Murphy's Life:

  • 1976 - Cillian Murphy is born on May 25th in Douglas, County Cork, Ireland.
  • 1996 - Begins studying law at University College Cork but drops out to pursue acting.
  • 1996-2001 - Performs in various theater productions in Dublin, including "Disco Pigs" and "The Country Boy."
  • 2002 - Makes his film debut in the thriller "Disco Pigs," reprising his role from the stage production.
  • 2003 - Gains critical acclaim for his role as Jim in Danny Boyle's post-apocalyptic film "28 Days Later."
  • 2005 - Stars in the psychological thriller "Red Eye" alongside Rachel McAdams, further establishing his presence in Hollywood.
  • 2006 - Collaborates with director Ken Loach on the critically acclaimed film "The Wind That Shakes the Barley," earning him widespread recognition.
  • 2010 - Portrays the enigmatic character Robert Fischer in Christopher Nolan's sci-fi blockbuster "Inception."
  • 2013 - Begins his role as Thomas Shelby, the lead character in the BBC series "Peaky Blinders," which becomes a major success.
  • 2017 - Reprises his role as Thomas Shelby in the fourth season of "Peaky Blinders."
  • 2018 - Stars in the thriller film "A Quiet Place" directed by John Krasinski, further expanding his range as an actor.
  • 2020 - Appears in the second season of "Peaky Blinders" as Thomas Shelby.
  • 2021 - Continues his role as Thomas Shelby in the sixth and final season of "Peaky Blinders."
